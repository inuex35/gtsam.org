const ACC & a ) const
inline

t.fold(f,a) computes [(f kN dN ... (f k1 d1 a)...)], where [k1 ... kN] are the keys of all bindings in [m], and [d1 ... dN] are the associated data.

The associated values are passed to [f] in reverse sort order
